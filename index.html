Why I want to become a Software Engineer

I want to become a Software Engineer because I have a passion with being up to date with the latest technology in the world and have a background of solving complex problems. I would like to work remotely in the future so that I can enjoy my work-life balance. One thing in particular that caught my attention was when I was able to program a toy car using code commands on the tablet that was provided by the building to run through an obstacle course going from point A to point B. I was watching other people trying to attempt this little puzzle, but had no luck in solving the problem. When it was my chance to go, I ran through so many errors on how to get the car started. But after trying, and not giving up. I finally found the command to make the car move. After making the car move, I had to program the car to turn, move forward, and reverse. It was a very tedious task, but a very rewarding end when I was able to get the car from destination A to destination B.
